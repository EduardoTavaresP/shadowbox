Shadowbox Examples

The examples listed below are provided to help you understand how to use Shadowbox to display a specific medium. Each example is as minimal as possible in order to demonstrate clearly how it works.

Name Description
Photo Open a photo by clicking on a link
Video Open a video by clicking on a link
Vimeo Open a Vimeo video by clicking on a link
YouTube Open a YouTube video by clicking on a link
Web Open a web page in an <iframe> by clicking on a link
Flash Open a Flash movie (SWF) by clicking on a link
Thumbnail Photo Open a photo by clicking on a thumbnail image
Thumbnail Gallery Open a gallery of photos by clicking on any one of a set of thumbnail images
